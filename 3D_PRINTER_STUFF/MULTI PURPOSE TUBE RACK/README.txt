# Summary

I designed multi-purpose racks for 50 and 15 mL falcon tubes, 1.5 and 2 mL Eppendorf tubes and for 8 in row PCR-tubes for different colleagues and want to share them with you.

Multi-Halter-A: 
-	2x 50 mL falcon tube
-	3x 15 mL falcon tube
-	15x 1.5 mL Eppendorf tube
-	9 rows PCR-tubes 

Multi-Halter-L:
-	2x 50 mL falcon tube
-	4x 15 mL falcon tube
-	6x 1.5 and 2 mL Eppendorf tube

Multi-Halter-Z:
-	4x 50 mL falcon tube
-	8x 15 mL falcon tube
-	9x 1.5 and 2 mL Eppendorf tube

Halter-50ml+15ml-Falcon+2ml-Eppis:
-	1x 50 mL falcon tube
-	1x 15 mL falcon tube
-	2x 1.5 and 2 mL Eppendorf tube

Others can be designed and uploaded on request.
