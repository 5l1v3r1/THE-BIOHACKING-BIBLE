Rose Bengal Agar (good for slowing growth of zygomycete colonies in a mixed culture when
making isolations, and for reducing bacterial contaminants)


1000 ml water
10 g D-glucose
5 g peptone
1 g KH2PO4
30 mg Rose Bengal (10 ml of a 3 g/l stock solution)
15 g agar
Autoclave and cool
Add 30 mg streptomycin, swirl to mix, and pour
