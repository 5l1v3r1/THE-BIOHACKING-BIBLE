Zenker liquid (microscope)

Hot melt 2.5 gr. of potassium dichromate and 1 gr. of sodium sulphate in 100 gr. of distilled water.

At 100 cm of this solution 5 gr. of corrosive sublimation. At the time of use, 5% acetic acid is added.

Fixation time: 6/10/24 hours

Wash in running water for a day and then pass in iodized alcohol (as in the acetic sublimated recipe).

Usually after fixation the material is washed under the tap from the water.
